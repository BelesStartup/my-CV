About Me

Hello! I’m Tesfay Hagos, a passionate software developer and entrepreneur based in Addis Ababa, Ethiopia. I hold a Master's degree in Electrical and Computer Engineering, with a focus on Machine Learning, Blockchain, and Fintech solutions. I am currently working on cutting-edge projects that span multiple domains including Blockchain, Fintech, and e-commerce.

With over five years of experience, I’ve contributed to various startups, taking on key roles in backend development using Golang and building highly scalable microservices. My expertise includes working on Fintech solutions such as integrating Stripe and building payment gateways from scratch. I have also developed e-commerce platforms, architected secure, fast, and reliable APIs, and deployed them using AWS and Docker.

In the Blockchain domain, I’ve been involved in building robust indexing platforms and secure transaction systems. My commitment to excellence drives me to constantly learn and apply new technologies to create efficient, reliable solutions that scale.

Apart from my professional work, I have a deep passion for teaching. As a university lecturer, I help the next generation of developers master emerging technologies and gain practical skills in programming and software development.
